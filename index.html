HOW INFLUX WORKS
METHODOLOGY · SIGNALS · ARCHITECTURE
📡
REAL-TIME PRICES
Commodity prices are fetched live from the Yahoo Finance API via a CORS proxy. Prices refresh on every analysis trigger, ensuring you always see current market conditions.
🧮
INFLATION PRESSURE SCORE
A composite 0–100 score computed from: price momentum vs. 30-day moving average, CPI trend data, supply chain stress index, and geopolitical risk weighting. Higher = more inflationary pressure.
📈
PRICE FORECAST MODEL
Uses a drift-adjusted stochastic model. The drift term (μ) is derived from recent price momentum and macro signals. Volatility (σ) is calibrated per-commodity using historical standard deviation proxies.
⚡
RISK CLASSIFICATION
HIGH risk: pressure > 70 or geopolitical score > 8. MODERATE: pressure 35–70. LOW: below 35. Classification updates dynamically with each new analysis run.
SIGNAL PIPELINE
01
PRICE INGESTION
Real-time spot price fetched from Yahoo Finance. Compared to embedded 30-day baseline to derive momentum direction and magnitude.
02
MACRO SIGNAL FUSION
CPI trend, supply chain index, and geopolitical risk score are loaded as static signal weights. These are calibrated to recent FRED and IMF data (updated quarterly in production).
03
PRESSURE SCORE COMPUTATION
Weighted average of 5 signals produces the 0–100 Inflation Pressure Score. Weights are derived from commodity-specific feature importance analysis (see dashboard).
04
MONTE CARLO FORWARD PROJECTION
Price path simulated with Geometric Brownian Motion. Drift term seeded from momentum + macro score. Volatility calibrated per commodity class. Forecast shown is the median of 200 simulated paths.
